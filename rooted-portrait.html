Rooted Portrait

A photography website that promotes body positivity, hendles, bookings, and displays portraits in staggered gallery.

Technologies Used:

HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Overview

Rooted Portraits is a body-positive photography booking platform that celebrates natural beauty by placing subjects within natural landscapes. The site invites users to embrace their authentic selves through artistic portrait photography.

Create a warm, inviting booking experience that reflects the brand's philosophy of natural beauty while showcasing the photographer's portfolio in a way that inspires potential clients.

The Challenge

A responsive, filterable gallery showcasing portrait work with smooth transitions and full-screen viewing options

User-friendly scheduling interface allowing clients to browse available sessions and book appointments directly

Flesh-toned color palette with organic shapes and nature-inspired imagery that reinforces the brand's message of embracing natural beauty

Design Reasonings

Color Scheme

I faced some design Challenges while building this site. I was going back and forth with the color scheme. Choosing skintones was the first thought. Thinking of browns and tans for the colors on my site felt blands in some respects. The solution was to lean more into flesh tones instead of skin, this includes the pinks and deep reds that you see throughout the site.


The idea for accent color can from wanting something that pops. Darker reds would blend in to much and wouldn't alert users to them. The golden yellow Color works because it harmonizes well with the pinks and reds and stands out perfectly!


Staggered Elements

The staggered images and elements that you see through the site came from the idea of growth and nature. The staggered images resembling the way branches grow up towards the sky. The offest columns are staggered to give the website a more oganic feel while still being organized. I stayed away from making the elements' borders circlar, or leaf-like , because it would take away from the images being displayed. The images need to be the main focus here so they need to be a "normal".

Results

Created an intuitive user experience that aligns with the brand's body-positive mission.
